Self Mailers & Tabbing Rules

The USPS has specific requirements for self-mailers to ensure they can be processed through automated equipment. Our team is well-versed in these regulations and will ensure your mailers comply with all postal requirements.

  • Proper tab placement based on mailer size and weight
  • Correct paper weight selection for different mail types
  • Address placement and formatting according to USPS guidelines
  • Barcode requirements for automation discounts
